About me

Howdy, I'm Reem, a software engineer currently stationed at Mozilla working directly on the Firefox browser.

I've lived in many different places, so I consider many different places home: Abu Dhabi, Doha, Beirut, Toronto, Brisbane, and Melbourne. As for where I really am, I've been lucky enough to consider Melbourne, Australia my home since 2023.

Like nearly every millennial with access to Web2.0 in the early 2000s, I dabbled with creating my own themes on Myspace and Tumblr using HTML and CSS. I decided to vocationally delve into web development after working in data science for a couple of mundane years and, remembering the creative excitement web development gave me in the past, I jumped ship from data. I'm nostalgic, see.
