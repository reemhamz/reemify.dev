About me

Howdy! I'm Reem and I'm currently (remotely) stationed at Mozilla as a senior web developer on the websites team. That's right, not only do I make websites, but I live and breathe them (until 5pm on weekdays).

I've lived in many places, so I consider many places home: Abu Dhabi, Doha, Beirut, Toronto, Brisbane, and Melbourne. As for where I'm (actually) stationed, I've been lucky enough to consider Melbourne, Australia my home since 2023, and I gotta say, for a coffee snob, it's kind of the dream.

Like nearly every millennial with access to Web2.0 in the early 2000s, I dabbled with creating my own themes on Myspace and Tumblr using HTML and CSS. I decided to delve professionally into web development after working in the data science field for a couple of mundane years and remembering the creative excitement web development brought me in the past. I'm nostalgic, see.

I've worked with many talented folks in the field that have inspired me endlessly. One exceptional person, in particular, challenged my biases, leading me to discover my true passion: web accessibility -- something I'm hoping to specialize in.
